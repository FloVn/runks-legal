Data Deletion Policy – RUNK Last updated: [Date] This Data Deletion Policy explains how users of the RUNK mobile application (“RUNK”, “the App”) can request the deletion of their personal data and how such requests are handled. 1. Scope This policy applies to all users of RUNK worldwide and covers personal data processed directly by RUNK as well as data imported from third-party services such as Garmin Connect. 2. Data Eligible for Deletion Upon request, RUNK will delete: - User account information (email, name, pseudonym, profile photo) - Sports and activity data imported from third-party platforms - Rankings, performance metrics, and derived statistics linked to the user account Aggregated and anonymized statistical data that no longer identifies an individual may be retained. 3. How to Request Data Deletion Users may request deletion of their data by: - Deleting their account directly within the application (when available), or - Contacting RUNK at: [privacy@runk.app] (placeholder) 4. Verification For security reasons, RUNK may verify the identity of the requesting user before processing the deletion request. 5. Processing Time Deletion requests are processed without undue delay and in accordance with applicable laws (including GDPR and CCPA/CPRA). 6. Third-Party Integrations If RUNK has received data from Garmin Connect or similar services, such data will be deleted from RUNK systems upon request. Users may also revoke access directly via the third-party platform. 7. Legal Retention Certain data may be retained if required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. 8. Updates ffected This Data Deletion Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be communicated appropriately. 9. Contact For any questions regarding data deletion, contact: [privacy@runk.app] (placeholder)
